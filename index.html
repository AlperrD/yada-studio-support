Is App Store Connect sync safe?

Yes. Your API credentials are stored exclusively in your device's secure Keychain. They are never sent to our servers.

How does the AI Background generator work?

Your text prompts are processed by Google Gemini AI. We do not upload your screenshots to our servers; the AI works based solely on your descriptions.
